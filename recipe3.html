Creamy White Sauce Pasta

25 mins Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • Cheese
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta and keep aside.
  2. Melt butter, add flour and cook.
  3. Add milk slowly and stir.
  4. Add cheese, salt, pepper.
  5. Mix pasta and serve hot.
