Chicken Biryani

60 mins Hard

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • Yogurt
  • Biryani spices
  • Ginger garlic paste

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices.
  2. Cook rice separately (70% cooked).
  3. Fry onions until golden.
  4. Cook chicken with spices.
  5. Layer rice and chicken, cook on low heat (dum).
