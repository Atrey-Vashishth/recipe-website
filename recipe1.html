Paneer Butter Masala

Preparation Time: 30 mins

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Spices

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan
  2. Add tomatoes and spices
  3. Add paneer
  4. Cook for 10 minutes
  5. Add cream and serve
