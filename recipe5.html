Fresh Veg Salad

10 mins Easy

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Lettuce
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Chop all vegetables.
  2. Add them to a bowl.
  3. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Mix well.
  5. Serve fresh.
