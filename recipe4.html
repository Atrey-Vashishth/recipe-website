Chocolate Cake

45 mins Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients.
  3. Add eggs and butter.
  4. Pour into baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30–35 minutes.
